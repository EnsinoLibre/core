Turn any topic into an interactive worksheet

EnsinoLibre is a free, open worksheet builder. Describe your lesson in the form below, copy the generated prompt into your favourite AI assistant, paste the result back — and get a digital worksheet you can use on screen or print on paper.

Create a worksheet Read the docs

📝 30 activity types

From multiple choice and gap-fill to flashcards, crosswords, branching scenarios and animated grammar — a full teaching-block library.

🤖 Bring your own AI

The generated prompt works with any capable assistant — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini or a local model. No API keys, no lock-in.

📖 Obsidian-ready docs

The whole documentation set is plain Markdown with wikilinks and callouts — drop it straight into your Obsidian vault.

🖨️ Screen or paper

Worksheets are interactive in the browser with tiered hints, and print cleanly as classic paper handouts.
