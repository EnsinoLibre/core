# Profile

**Profile** in the [[platform-overview|teacher platform]] holds your own details (name, email, school, subjects, bio) and doubles as the home for whole-workspace actions that don't belong to any single entity.

## Your details

A plain form — name, email, school/institution, subjects, bio. These aren't just display fields: your name and subjects appear in [[mcp-connect|an MCP agent]]'s workspace context, and the bio is exactly the kind of teaching-philosophy note worth actually filling in once.

## Seed knowledge base

Bulk-add files — the same flow available from the [[resources-knowledge-base|Resources]] page, repeated here for convenience. Full mechanic in [[knowledge-seeding]].

## Import from Google Classroom

Bring in existing classes, rosters and materials from a real Google Takeout export — see [[google-classroom-import]] for the step-by-step.

## Obsidian vault export

**⬇ Download vault (.zip)** turns your entire workspace — classrooms, students, worksheets, resources, live classes — into linked Markdown notes with YAML frontmatter and wikilinks, the same shape as this documentation. Open the unzipped folder in Obsidian, or hand it to an AI agent for a token-efficient read of the whole workspace at once. See [[obsidian-vault]] for what's inside and why.

## Workspace data

A reminder that your workspace lives in Supabase, synced across devices — with a manual **Refresh from server** if you ever suspect you're looking at a stale view.
